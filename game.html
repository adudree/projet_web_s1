This is a game where you must help the character to find a place with the weather she needs.
You can enter the name of a city on Earth, and the character will be transported in a place with the same current weather.
For example, if you enter "New York" and there's rain in New York now, the character will be wet.
Read what she says and find out what weather she needs.

Enjoy
